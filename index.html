The Project

The Archimedes team at Holy Cross is presently examining two texts of Archimedes: the tenth century Codex C as preserved in the Archimedes Palimpsest, and the sixteenth century Codex Bodmer 8. This year's project will compare On the Sphere and Cylinder as it is preserved in each codex.
